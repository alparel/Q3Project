Who Am I?

My name is Adrian Josef Parel and I am a student currently studying in OB Montessori Center, Sta. Ana branch.


What webpages do I usually make?

Webpages that I usually make provide tips to avoid accidents and what to do before and during a disaster.


What made me create these webpages?

The story starts when I was studying about disasters. I learned that the Philippines, where I live, is actually part of the Pacific Ring of Fire, which means we are prone to frequent earthquakes and volcanic eruptions. So to address this problem, I decided to make a website that helps people who are prone to the negative effects of accidents/disasters, so that they can ensure their safety and the safety of others.
